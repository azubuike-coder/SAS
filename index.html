About SAS

Strategic Agricultural Solutions Limited (SAS) is committed to providing sustainable and innovative solutions in the agricultural sector. We aim to improve productivity, profitability, and sustainability through cutting-edge technology and expert services.

Founded by Tony Alagbile, SAS thrives on strong leadership, years of experience, and a deep passion for agriculture. Working alongside him are Joy Azubuike and James Peters, key members of our team, who bring invaluable expertise and dedication to the company's mission.
